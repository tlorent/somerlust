FAQ

Directions

To get to Park Somerlust, take the metro to Spaklerweg. Or have a nice stroll along the Amstel from Amstel Station, reachable by train or metro.

Time

Somerlust Festival is on the 5th of August 2018. It starts at 13:00 and ends at 23:00. Please leave the area quietly and respect the neighbours.

Food

We have some fantastic food trucks located at the festival, where you can get your pulled pork, seaweed burgers, fruit salads, and falafel wraps.

What can I bring?

Obviously your good spirit and respect for other people. So no flammable objects, nothing that could accidentally kill someone, no drugs, no drinks. You know the drill.

Contact

Social Media

You can send us a message via our social media. We will answer as soon as humanly possible.

Email

Urgent question? Need to know if you can bring your dog, or how bad we will punish you if you bring in your own drinks? Email us at info@somerlust.com.
